Our Mission & Vision

Vision:- The college aims at ameliorating the condition of the people who are socially oppressed and traditionally marginalised by spreading the general education in the area where many SC/ST and poor agricultural families co-exist, thus continuing the legacy of ‘Bankura Sammilani Trust Body’, as established in 1911 for overall development of the area of Bankura District. To train the students’ mind towards innovative thinking, because true education, our vision enunciates, does not consist insipid learning of certain dull facts.
